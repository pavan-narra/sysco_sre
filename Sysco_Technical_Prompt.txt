If asked "How would you solve X hypothetical production issue at Sysco?"

Approach:
1. Intake & Triage: Check dashboards (Datadog/Splunk), acknowledge the alert, gather the team.
2. Isolate: Look at logs, APM traces to identify if it's network, database, or application code.
3. Mitigate: Roll back the deployment, scale up resources, or apply a hotfix to restore service quickly.
4. RCA & Process: Post-mortem, update runbooks, improve the alert so it doesn't happen again.
