# Topics to Cover for the Sysco SRE Interview

Based on the job description, your resume, and the provided interview prompts, here are the key topics you should be prepared to discuss during your HR and Technical interviews for the Site Reliability Engineer role at Sysco.

## 1. HR & Behavioral Topics (Focus: Erica Jacobs)
*   **Tell Me About Yourself:** Rehearse a crisp 60-second summary highlighting your 6+ years as an SRE, AWS expertise, APM tooling specialization, and collaborative mindset.
*   **Why Sysco?** Connect your goals to Sysco's mission ("at the heart of food and service") and global scale (e.g., Sysco Shop & API Central handling ~40B transactions).
*   **Why Are You Looking?** Focus on seeking a full-time role in a dynamic organization with a hybrid model, wanting to apply enterprise cloud expertise at scale.
*   **Behavioral Scenarios (STAR Method):**
    *   **Biggest Challenge / Complex RCA:** Preparing a story on identifying a critical issue, taking action (e.g., Datadog observability), and resolving it.
    *   **Conflict / Stakeholder Management:** Discuss aligning with DevOps, Engineering, or customers to implement process improvements without ego.
*   **Cultural Fit:** Emphasize clear communication, empathy, diverse perspectives, and collaboration.

## 2. General SRE & Operations (Focus: Surath Perera)
*   **Incident Management Lifecycle:** From intake, triage, isolating the issue, mitigating, to resolving (MTTR, SLA, OLA tracking).
*   **Root Cause Analysis (RCA):** Explain your methodology for post-mortems and eliminating recurring problems.
*   **Cross-Shore Collaboration:** Discuss your experience partnering with Level 1 and Level 2 offshore teams (like Sysco LABS Sri Lanka), aligning with ITSM standards.
*   **Documentation & Knowledge Sharing:** Updating runbooks, creating operational documentation, and standardizing RCA processes.

## 3. DevOps, Automation, & Architecture (Focus: Chaminda Bandara)
*   **CI/CD & Deployment:** Workflows, Python scripting, Docker containerization, Jenkins, and release automation.
*   **Infrastructure as Code (IaC):** Your experience with Terraform, AWS cloud integrations, and ensuring infrastructure scalability.
*   **Application Integration:** Understanding of Java/React.js architectures and how you integrate SRE practices (shifting left on reliability) with developer workflows.
*   **Incident Mitigation:** Rolling back deployments, applying hotfixes, and scaling resources fast.

## 4. Performance, Tooling, & Reliability (Focus: David Kosub)
*   **Observability & APM Tools:** Deep dive into how you use Datadog, Splunk, and New Relic for proactive monitoring, alerting, and log analysis.
*   **Performance Tuning:** Identifying system bottlenecks, optimizing execution times, and lowering application latency.
*   **Enterprise Cloud Migration & Scaling:** Discuss your work migrating to AWS, scaling databases, and designing high-availability systems.
*   **Data Platform Context:** Be prepared for potential queries related to handling large data volumes, given Datadog usage and Sysco's massive transactional volume.

## 5. Key Anchor Experiences to Rotate Through
*   **HealthPartners:** Incident management strategies, RCA, Datadog/Splunk, partnering with L1 & engineering.
*   **University of Houston:** 99.9% availability monitoring, AWS cloud migrations, and creating knowledge base articles.
*   **Netcracker:** DevOps automation (Python, Docker, CI/CD), checking logs, reducing latency, New Relic APM, and high-availability production support.
